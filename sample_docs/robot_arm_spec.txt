TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION: 6-AXIS INDUSTRIAL ROBOT ARM SYSTEM
Version: 2.1 | Date: 2024-01
Prepared by: Engineering Department

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
This document describes the technical specification for a 6-axis industrial robot arm 
system to be installed in the automotive assembly plant, Building 7, Line 3. The robot 
will perform welding, part pick-and-place, and quality inspection tasks in a semi-automated 
collaborative manufacturing cell.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
2. SYSTEM SPECIFICATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
Mechanical:
- Axes: 6 degrees of freedom
- Payload capacity: 150 kg (including end-effector)
- Maximum reach: 2,800 mm
- Repeatability: ±0.05 mm
- Maximum joint speed: 180°/sec
- End-effector: Quick-change tooling plate + welding torch + vision system

Power:
- Supply voltage: 480V AC, 3-phase, 60 Hz
- Control cabinet power: 120V AC, single-phase
- Peak power consumption: 22 kW
- Operating current: 28 A (RMS)
- Control voltage: 24V DC (safety-rated)

Control System:
- PLC: Safety-rated (SIL 2 capable)
- Servo drives: 6x integrated servo amplifiers
- Safety controller: Dedicated safety module
- HMI: 15" touchscreen pendant
- Communication: EtherNet/IP, Profinet
- Programming: ISO 9283 compliant

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
3. INSTALLATION ENVIRONMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
- Location: Indoor manufacturing floor
- Ambient temperature: 5°C to 45°C
- Humidity: 10% to 95% non-condensing
- Contamination: Metal swarf, weld spatter, cutting fluid mist
- Noise level: ~85 dB(A) at 1m during operation
- Floor loading: Robot base: 2,400 kg static load
- Installation area: 6m x 8m fenced cell
- Operator access: Required for teaching, maintenance, and part loading

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
4. OPERATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
Automatic Mode:
- Fully automated 24/7 production cycles
- Average cycle time: 45 seconds
- Part throughput: ~80 parts/hour
- Operating speed: Up to 2.0 m/s TCP speed

Manual/Teaching Mode:
- Speed limited to 250 mm/s (teach pendant enable switch)
- Requires operator entry into robot cell
- 2-person procedure required for speeds above 250 mm/s

Maintenance Mode:
- Full power isolation required for all maintenance
- Estimated maintenance frequency: Daily inspection, 500-hour PM

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
5. KNOWN HAZARDS (PRELIMINARY)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
- High-speed moving arm capable of causing fatal crush injuries
- 480V electrical supply — arc flash hazard
- Welding torch: UV radiation, fumes, spatter, fire hazard
- Heavy component handling during installation/maintenance
- Pinch points at all 6 joints
- Possible hydraulic leaks (counterbalance system)
- Software/control failure causing unexpected movement
- Operator entry during teach mode

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
6. EXISTING SAFEGUARDS (PLANNED)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════
- Steel perimeter fence (1.8m high) with interlocked access gates
- Emergency stop buttons at all access points and on teach pendant
- Safety PLC with redundant safety outputs
- Teach pendant with 3-position enabling device
- Weld fume extraction system
- Warning lights and audible alarm for auto mode
